UCE TINDIVANAM · BATCH 15 · 2022–2026
Overview WATER QUALITY SYSTEM
🎯

Predict Water Quality

Uses Random Forest to predict a Water Quality Score (0–1) and classify water as Good or Bad with 95% accuracy.

🏭

Identify Pollution Sources

Rule-based approach detects Industrial, Agricultural, and Domestic sewage pollution from water parameters.

💡

Give Recommendations

Provides actionable solutions to improve water quality based on detected parameter issues.

How It Works
1

Upload Dataset or Enter Parameters

Upload a CSV file with water quality data or manually enter 11 parameters in the Predict tab.

2

ML Model Predicts Score

Random Forest Regressor computes Water Quality Score. Classifier labels water as Good (≥0.5792) or Bad.

3

Pollution Detection & Solutions

System detects high BOD, COD, Coliform values, identifies pollution source, and suggests improvements.
